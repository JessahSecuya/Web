Our Mission

We are Woof World, a family-owned kennel that breeds and shows Labradors and Poodles. Our mission is to:

  • Produce healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies that meet the breed standards and excel in the show ring, as well as in the home.
  • Provide lifetime support and guidance to our puppy buyers, and maintain a close relationship with them.
  • Promote responsible dog ownership and educate the public about the benefits and challenges of owning a Labrador or a Poodle.
  • Contribute to the preservation and improvement of our breeds, by following ethical breeding practices and participating in health testing and research programs.

We love our dogs, and we treat them as part of our family. They live with us in our home, and enjoy a spacious and secure backyard. They also accompany us on our travels, adventures, and activities. We believe that dogs are not just pets, but companions and friends.

If you are interested in adding a Labrador or a Poodle to your family, or if you want to learn more about our breeds, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you and answer any questions you may have.

Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope you enjoy browsing through our photos and stories.
